Sylvie Bernier will retire from diving in December 1984, only a few months after her Olympic debut. In 1985, she will receive the Order of Canada. She will serve as "chef de mission" for Team Canada at the Olympic Games in 2008 and twice as assistant "chef de mission" in 2006 and 2012.
