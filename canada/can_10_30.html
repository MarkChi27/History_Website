October 30, 1995

Quebec voters have their say in the referendum on Quebec sovereignty. By 50.6%, they vote “No” to the ballot question: “Do you agree that Quebec should become sovereign after having made a formal offer to Canada for a new economic and political partnership within the scope of the bill respecting the future of Quebec and of the agreement signed on June 12, 1995?”

In this referendum, voter turnout is a record 93.52%.
