Canadaâ€™s Parliament announces that a national library will be created on January 1, 1953, following the passage of the National Library Act. The mandate of the National Library of Canada will be to maintain a wide range of written records related to Canadian heritage.

The first National Librarian of Canada will be Dominion Archivist William Kaye Lamb. He will play a decisive role in the development of this cultural institution.
