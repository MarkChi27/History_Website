May 29, 1914

The passenger ship S.S. Empress of Ireland sinks beneath the waters of the St. Lawrence River, near Rimouski, Quebec, 14 minutes after colliding with the collier S.S. Storstad.

Dense fog and confusion about the location and direction of the ships leads to the collision. Only 465 of the 1,477 people aboard the Empress of Ireland survive.
