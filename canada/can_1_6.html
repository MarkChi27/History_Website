January 6, 1937

Brother André dies at the age of 91. He was the founder of St. Joseph's Oratory in Montréal, which has the third-largest basilica dome in the world. During a seven-day period, over a million people will file past Brother André's casket to honour him. He will be canonized by Pope Benedict XVI on October 17, 2010.

St. Joseph's Oratory of Mount Royal began as a modest chapel, built at Brother André's request in the early 1900s. Its status would be raised to that of minor basilica in 1956. Today, St. Joseph's Oratory receives over a million visitors a year from every part of the world.
