May 18, 1970

The Guess Who’s hit song “No Sugar Tonight” reaches number one on the U.S. Billboard charts. The rock band from Winnipeg had numerous #1 hits, including “These Eyes” and “American Woman,” throughout the 1970s. In 1987, the Guess Who will be inducted into the Canadian Music Hall of Fame alongside Joni Mitchell, Glenn Gould, Paul Anka, Hank Snow and others. In 2002, Governor General Adrienne Clarkson will present the five band members, Randy Bachman, Burton Cummings, Garry Peterson, Donnie McDougall and Bill Wallace, with the Governor General’s Performing Arts Award.
