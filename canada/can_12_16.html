Henri Menier, a wealthy French chocolatier, buys Anticosti Island in Quebec for $125,000. He wants to establish a private sports preserve on this island in the Gulf of St. Lawrence.

Menier will import Virginia white-tailed deer and other species to the island for his hunting paradise. He will also build a villa, Ch√¢teau Menier. With much money and effort, he will develop the island, building Port-Menier and other villages.
