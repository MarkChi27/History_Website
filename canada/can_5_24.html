May 24, 2000

The Walkerton tragedy claims its first four victims: three adults and a baby. Contaminated well water will lead to the deaths of seven residents of Walkerton, Ontario, and sicken 2,500 others in the small town of approximately 5,000 people.

Residents had reported symptoms of E. coli infections since May 13. A boil-water advisory was issued on May 21. The individuals responsible, the Koebel brothers, will eventually enter into a plea bargain and will be found guilty of “common nuisance.”
