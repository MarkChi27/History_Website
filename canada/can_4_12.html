April 12, 1980

Terry Fox begins his Marathon of Hope in Newfoundland. His goal is to cross Canada to raise money for cancer research. Having lost his right leg to the disease, he will run for 143 days on an artificial leg made of fibreglass and steel. Fox will cover 5,373 km through the Maritimes, Quebec and Ontario. Health problems forced him to stop near Thunder Bay, Ontario. Fox will die on June 28, 1981, one month short of his 23rd birthday.
