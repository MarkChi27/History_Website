August 2, 1914

Québécois poet and singer-songwriter Félix Leclerc is born in La Tuque, Quebec.

He will be considered a pioneer in his field and become one of the greatest ambassadors of Québécois song in the Francophonie. During his career, Félix Leclerc will win numerous awards and receive many honours. His musical legacy will include over 150 songs.

On February 7, 2014, he will be officially designated as a Quebec historical figure by the Quebec government for having helped to shape the collective identity of Quebecers through his body of work.

At its annual gala, the Association québécoise de l'industrie du disque, du spectacle et de la vidéo (ADISQ) celebrates accomplishments with Félix Awards, named after the poet and singer-songwriter.
