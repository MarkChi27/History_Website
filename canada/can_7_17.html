July 17, 1976

Montréal hosts the first Olympic Games in Canada. Canadian athletes will win 11 medals. Two of the most memorable athletes from the games: Romanian gymnast Nadia Comaneci and American decathlete Bruce Jenner.

Montréal mayor Jean Drapeau, determined to bring the world to his city, started his Olympic bid on a visit to Lausanne, Switzerland, in 1963.

Drapeau was also involved in bringing Montréal the Métro subway system, the Expo 67 world's fair and the Expos baseball team.
