November 26, 1917

The National Hockey League (NHL) is established during a meeting of representatives of the National Hockey Association at Montréal’s Windsor Hotel.

The owners of five professional hockey teams, the Montreal Canadiens, Montreal Wanderers, Ottawa Senators, Quebec City Bulldogs and Toronto Arenas, decide to create a new league. With the First World War raging, many hockey players had been conscripted to fight.

The season will open on December 19, 1917. The first two games will feature the Montreal Canadiens against the Ottawa Senators, and the Toronto Arenas versus the Montreal Wanderers. Both Montréal teams will win these first two contests.
