October 19, 1987

This is “Black Monday” on the New York Stock Exchange, as stock prices plummet spectacularly. In a single day, the U.S. Dow Jones Industrial Average falls by 22%. The stock market crash sends shock waves around the world.

At the Toronto Stock Exchange, the TSE 300 Composite Index drops sharply by more than 11%. Single-day losses in Canada amount to $37 billion. It will be two years before the market index returns to pre-crash levels.
