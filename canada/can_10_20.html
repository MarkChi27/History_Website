October 20, 1961

Mayor Jean Drapeau announces that Montreal will have a subway: the Metro.

He presents a draft of the project, which consists of three lines: line 1 (green) will run east-west, line 2 (orange) will run north-south, and line 3, which will run underneath Mount Royal.

Line 3 will be rejected; instead, a new line will go under the St. Lawrence River, line 4 (yellow). This line will service the site of Expo 67. The Metro will run on pneumatic tires and be completely underground. Each station will have its own distinctive architecture and design.
