April 28, 1967

Montreal welcomes the world at the official opening of the 1967 World’s Fair. Expo 67 is a key event marking the 100th anniversary of Canadian Confederation.

From April 28 to October 29, 1967, over 50 million visitors will throng to Îles Sainte-Hélène and Notre-Dame. The visitors show “passports” to enjoy the rides, shows and exhibitions in more than 90 pavilions from 62 countries and private sponsors.

Expo 67: a huge success and a memorable event for an entire generation.
