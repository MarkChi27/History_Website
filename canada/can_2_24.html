February 24, 1942

As the Second World War rages, the Government of Canada enforces the War Measures Act and orders the internment of Japanese Canadians. In all, 26,000 people, including women and children, will be forced to leave their homes.

On September 22, 1988, Canadian Prime Minister Brian Mulroney will issue a public apology on behalf of the government to all survivors of Japanese origin.
