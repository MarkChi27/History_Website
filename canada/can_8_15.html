August 15, 1884

The Acadian flag is adopted at the Second Acadian National Convention in Miscouche, Prince Edward Island. It has blue, white and red vertical bands like the French flag, but the Acadian flag also has a yellow star in the blue band to symbolize the sea and Roman Catholicism. The flag will fly for the first time the day after the convention.
