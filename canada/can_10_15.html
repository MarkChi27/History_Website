October 15, 1989

Brantford, Ontario’s hockey great Wayne Gretzky, playing for the Los Angeles Kings, scores two goals and obtains one assist in a game against his former team, the Edmonton Oilers. In reaching 1,851 points, Gretzky surpasses Gordie Howe for the most career points in the National Hockey League (NHL). “The Great One” will finish his career with 2,857 points, some 900 more than any other professional player.
