December 30, 2009

A terrible explosion in Kandahar, Afghanistan, kills four Canadian soldiers: Private Garrett William Chidley, Corporal Zachery McCormack, Sergeant George Miok and Sergeant Kirk Taylor. Canadian reporter Michelle Lang from the Calgary Herald also dies in the explosion. An improvised explosive device blows up their vehicle as they travel in a convoy through the city.

Michelle Lang is the first journalist to die in Afghanistan since Canada deployed its forces there in 2002.
